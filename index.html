I received my Ph.D. degree from Shanghai University in 2022, supervised by Prof. Zhi Liu. From July 2021 to June 2022, I was a Visiting Ph.D. Student at the Media & Interactive Computing Lab (MICL-CVL), School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, supervised by Prof. Weisi Lin (IEEE/IET Fellow and Honorary Fellow of SIET (Singapore Institute of Engineering Technologists)). From June 2022 to June 2024, I worked as a Postdoctoral Researcher with Prof. Xinpeng Zhang at Shanghai University, China. My research interests include saliency detection, multi-modal image processing, and image/video segmentation.
